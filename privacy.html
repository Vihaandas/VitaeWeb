Vitae Privacy Policy

 Effective: Jan 2, 2026

Your data. Your pace. Your control.

Vitae is designed for emotional awareness through journaling, meditation experiences, and an optional AI Assistant. This policy explains what information we collect, how it’s used, and your choices.

Contact: vitae.contact.app@gmail.com

1) Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Profile details (optional): name, age, gender, email, and a profile photo/avatar if you choose.
  • Journal entries and notes you type into the app.
  • Meditation selections to support the experience.
  • AI Assistant messages: what you type into the assistant and the assistant’s responses.

We do not intentionally collect: contacts, location, or microphone recordings.

2) How We Use Information

  • To display your profile and personalize the app experience.
  • To save your journal content and app settings.
  • To provide meditation experiences and in-app features.
  • To generate responses when you use the AI Assistant.

3) AI Assistant & Third-Party Processing

If you use the AI Assistant, the text you submit is sent to an AI service provider to generate a response. Only what you type into the AI Assistant is sent.

Important: Vitae is not medical advice and is not a substitute for professional care. AI responses may be inaccurate.

4) Data Storage

Vitae stores your profile and journal data locally on your device (unless you choose to share text through the AI Assistant). If you delete the app, local data may be removed by your device.

5) Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

  • We share AI Assistant text with the AI provider only when you submit it for a response.
  • We may share information if required by law or to protect safety and security.

6) Data Retention & Security

Local app data remains on your device unless you delete it or uninstall the app. We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

7) Children’s Privacy

Vitae is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us to request deletion.

8) Your Choices

  • You can edit your profile information in the app.
  • You can delete journal entries within the app.
  • You can stop using the AI Assistant at any time by not submitting messages.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We’ll update the effective date at the top when changes are made.
